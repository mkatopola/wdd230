Join the Chamber

Membership Levels

NP Membership

No fee, suitable for non-profit organizations.

  • Access to community events
  • Basic directory listing
  • Networking opportunities
  • Participation in volunteer activities
  • Monthly community newsletter
  • Invitations to special non-profit forums

Bronze Membership

Entry-level membership with basic benefits.

Fee: $100/year

  • Access to all events and seminars
  • Monthly newsletter
  • Discounted event fees
  • Basic advertising opportunities
  • Access to member-only online content
  • Eligibility for committee participation

Silver Membership

Mid-level membership with additional benefits.

Fee: $250/year

  • Enhanced directory listing
  • Free entry to networking events
  • Access to online resources and webinars
  • Special training sessions
  • Priority event seating
  • Opportunities for collaborative projects

Gold Membership

Premium membership with maximum benefits.

Fee: $500/year

  • Priority support and consultation
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities
  • Complimentary tickets to major events
  • Spotlight positions on the home page
  • VIP access to special events
  • Customizable advertising packages
